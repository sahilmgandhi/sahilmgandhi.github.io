Sahil's Movie Ratings

I love watching and rating movies in my free time, and here are some of the movies that I have rated recently. I try to update this list within a few days of watching a new film!

My Favorite Movie?

So what is my favorite movie?



Drumroll ... it's the Dark Knight! I've watched "The Dark Knight" more than 15 times till date and always find something new every time that I watch the movie. Heath Ledger's Joker, Christain Bale's Batman and Hans Zimmer's music when put together are a masterpiece, and every other actor in the movie perfectly portrays the chaos and turmoil that exists in Gotham City. The other two films in this series, "Batman Begins" and "The Dark Knight Rises" are also notable, though this one stole the show.
